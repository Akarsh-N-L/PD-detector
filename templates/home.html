Information about Parkinson's.

Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects movement. It occurs when the nerve cells (neurons) that produce a chemical called dopamine, which is responsible for controlling movement, become damaged and die.

There is currently no cure for Parkinson's disease, but treatments such as medications, surgery, and lifestyle changes can help manage the symptoms and improve quality of life. Read More
